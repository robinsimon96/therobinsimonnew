    • LANCE SNACKS

    Concepted, art directed, and designed content for Lance Snacks social media platforms. Utilized different styles based on platform performance.

    Personified Sandwich Crackers

    In an effort to create Facebook and Twitter content that aligned more directly with the fun loving, kid friendly persona of Lance I created animations featuring personified sandwich crackers. Their actions are meant to mirror activities and events that Lance families can relate to, morphing their day to day lives from back to school shopping to summer time fun into ownable content for the brand.

    Instagram Stories
